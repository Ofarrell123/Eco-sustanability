What is sustanability and why is it so important?

First of all, sustanability refers to the practice of protecting our environment through sustainable actions and preserving our natural resources. Sustainability is a concept that has been around for a long time, but it has only recently been adopted. Sustanibility is important as it ensures we do not eratically consume all of the resources on earth, leaving it dry. Below is a video which explores the importance of this topic further.

What will happen if we neglect sutanable practices ?
